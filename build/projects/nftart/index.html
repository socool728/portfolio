The problem

In 2021, NFT Inator began a project to build an entirely new platform to from the ground up to serve as the most powerful toolkit for users to accelerate design and development of procedurally generated NFT projects. there were a number of user experience and user interface problems to solve in the process of moving the platform to React. The primary goals for the project were reducing barriers to collaboration, and making the platform both easier for new users, but with plenty of room to scale for advanced users.
